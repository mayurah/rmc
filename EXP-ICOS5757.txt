Refinitiv Export Code   : NL 

Export Note                   : This export code means ‘Not Licensable’ and 
                                is applied to items that can be exported 
                                ‘no license required’, under both EU and US 
                                regulations. Exports of NL items can be 
                                made to all countries except embargoed 
                                countries (Cuba, Iran, Sudan, Syria, and 
                                North Korea).
